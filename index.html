Our Story

From Structure
to Sanctuary

RK Enterprise was born from a simple belief: a container is not just steel — it's a canvas. We transform industrial-grade shipping containers into beautifully crafted spaces that people love to live in, work from, and build businesses around.

With factories in Bardhaman (West Bengal) and Faridabad (Delhi NCR), we serve clients across India with speed, precision, and an obsession for quality.
